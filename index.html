About the Website

Based on personal experience, web developers rarely make applications solely from scratch. Rather, we tend to expand what's already created and perfected, allowing us to unleash our creativity from the latest possible technological discovery. I guess you could say that we're just really good thieves. But why is our thievery legal?

This website is dedicated to the principles of legal development; which is the process of creating web applications while following the license statement few of us ever actually read.

For it's what that statement actually says, and how well we adhere to it, that determines just how good of a thief we are.
